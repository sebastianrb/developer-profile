About Me

Overview

Welcome! I am a React-focused front-end web developer. I currently work as the lead developer for iPullRank, a digital agency based in New York City.

I grew up in the suburbs of New York City and moved to Colorado when I was a teenager. After high school, I attended Dartmouth College and studied Economics and Arabic, and furthermore was fortunate enough to study both Arabic and Spanish abroad in Morocco and Mexico, respectively. Outside of web development, my interests and passions include photography (the header image is one of my photos), travel, cooking, languages, running, skiing, and weightlifting.
